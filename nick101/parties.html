Salon Nick 101 - Our Birthday Packages

Salon Nick 101 pampers young ladies (3 to 16 years) with manicures, pedicures, make-up and more, as well as little gents (3 to 11 years) with face painting, hair colouring and more. Celebrate your child's birthday at Salon Nick 101 with a personalized and stylish party designed just for you!

Choose below from a variety of fun spa party packages of a duration of two hours that accommodate all ages. Each party consists of 75 minutes in the spa room and 45 minutes in our decorated party room.

Feel free to bring snacks, drinks and plates/cups/silverware for your guests. The place is well equipped with a fridge and freezer for storing any food you have brought.

Please note that we can always customize any spa party to meet your desires and your birthday child's wishes! It will be our pleasure to accommodate you with the number of kids you would like to invite as well as the activities you pick for the party at a pro-rated cost.

Guests must reserve at least two weeks in advance to be guaranteed a seat. A $100.00 non-refundable deposit fee is required to confirm your party date, with full payment due on the day of the party.At least one adult must be present during the activity (3 to 11 years).

For birthday parties inquiries or if you have any questions about our spa parties and events, please contact Nicole at 613-864-4041 or email us at salonnick101@hotmail.com." If you have been in touch with us by email, please check your junk/spam folder and if you cannot see a reply from us, please call us (613-864-4041) and we will follow up.

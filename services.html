COVID-19: Policies and Procedures ⯆

We are following strict guidelines to ensure the safety of our clients and staff due to COVID-19.

  • Upon arrival kindly remain in your car and call us at 613-596-6292 to let you know when you may come in.
  • When you come into the salon please wear a face mask. We recommend a disposable one if you're receiving a color service. Will have disposable masks available upon request.
  • Please wash/sanitize your hands immediately upon entering the salon.
  • Please keep any unnecessary items in your vehicle. Bring only the essentials - keys, phone and payment method.
  • We will be discontinuing all beverage services in the salon as well as removing all magazines.
  • Keep family/friends safe at home. Only one client is allowed per station.

Thank you all for your patience and understanding. Looking forward to serving you all.

Salon Nick - Services

Looking for high quality and personal hair service? You are at the right place! At Salon Nick, will give you the attention and professional service you deserve. We offer a full selection of salon services to make you look and feel unique.

Women's

Hair Cut

Get styled the way you want it! Our knowledgeable staff who knows which look works for each of our clients is ready to serve you with years of experience.

Blow Dry Style

Looking to transform your hair to something much more interesting, our hairstylists are happy to help your hair having volume with the kind of gentle, glossy curves that make you look like celebrities!

Up-do

Up-do hairstyles are perfect for special occasions such as a prom, weddings and parties. Also Up-dos are a fabulous hairstyle solution for long, medium and even short hair. It is perfect for women who want to make a statement!

Hair Treatment

At Salon Nick we offer the best in hair care services and treatments. We use Joico Collection that is suitable for the hair care weak and exhausting in order to protect the hair and make it stronger.

Colour

We are proud at Salon Nick that our hairstylists have experience with various techniques such as the traditional foils, solid colours, balayage, etc. we are always available to provide you with professional beauty and wellness tips to ensure you have the hair colour you are dreaming of!

Highlights / Lowlights

Not sure if lowlights or highlights is the right technique for you, your hairstylist at Salon Nick is happy to help you. Unlike highlights, which are accents of colour that are lighter than the base colour and creates volume, lowlights are darker strands of colour that are interlaced into the hair.

Perms

Obtain the hair of you dream with a permanent wave, commonly called a “perm”. Perms can provide a lot of texture and volume to you hair. Our professional hairstylists treat your hair with ISO Perm to alter the structure and permanently wave or curl you hair that lasts approximately three to six months.

Men's

Hair Cut

Get styled the way you want it! Our knowledgeable staff who knows which look works for each of our clients is ready to serve you with years of experience.

Colour

In need of men's hair colour? Colouring is no longer reserved for women, and more and more men dare to dye hair. We are proud at Salon Nick that our hairstylists have experience with various techniques and are here to help you covering up those gray roots. We are always available to provide you with professional service for healthy-looking hair.

Highlights

Highlights are very popular for men nowadays. Highlights are accents of colour that are lighter than the base colour and create volume. There are many colors that can be used for highlighting, your hairstylist at Salon Nick is happy to help you.

Children's

Hair Cut

Kids and adults are welcome at Salon Nick. Our experienced hairdressers are ready to give your child a great haircut in a fun environment! Every effort is made to make it a fun and comfortable experience.

Temporary Hair Colours

Hair colors are a rising trend for kids and teens.. Now have your fun! No strings or damage attached! At Salon Nick we use temporary hair colours that wash out easily with shampoo and do not damage your hair. Check our products section for more details.
